House Energy Estimation System

YYC Hackathon 2023

The Accommodation Energy Estimation System is a Python tool that estimates household energy usage based on user-input appliance usage patterns. It calculates approximate electricity and water consumption, providing insights into energy costs and environmental impact. The program serves as an educational tool for increasing energy awareness in residential settings.

Github

Flight Management System

ENSF 337

The Flight Management System is a C++ program for managing airline flight information. It offers features like displaying seat maps, managing passenger data, and saving information to files. Users interact with the system through a menu-based interface, allowing them to add or remove passengers and view flight details. The program demonstrates object-oriented programming principles and file handling in C++, making it suitable for small-scale airline management or as an educational tool for learning C++ concepts.

Sail Car

ENGG 200

Designing a wind-powered "Sail Car" to carry 100 marbles across 150 cm. My team and I have just two hours to brainstorm, build, and test our creation using a limited selection of materials and a tight budget. We need to document our design process, manage resources carefully, and present our solution. It's a race against time to create a functional Sail Car that meets all the requirements. We're determined to rise to the challenge and make our design work, putting our engineering skills to the test in this hands-on project.
